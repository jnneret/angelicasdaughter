Jayla is a junior at the University of San Francisco studying design and film. She is currently working in the media department at USF alongside other creatives with a passion for video and audio production.

Jayla is a writer, designer, photographer, and videographer who works in many mediums. While always eager to create and learn something new she primarily works with print, graphic design, typography, motion graphics, mixed media, and film.

A proud Haitian-Italian-American who was raised in Massachusetts she has goals to create art that means something and is representative. Growing up Jayla was interested in film and had a deep love of art; as she grew to be passionate about social justice movements the two fused. She often creates art inspired by the struggles to fight for liberation as well as the joy and beauty of being.

She is motivated by social justice movements and the artists around her with goals to continue creating, learning, advocating, and contributing art to the world.
