The Oppressors Langage, Ancestral Strength (from Bell Hooks on the Language of Power)

Course: Typography - Fall 2023 - 12” x 12” Poster

Here are two different typographic posters. One uses a heavy-weight black typeface, Futura, on a white background and the other uses a light-weight white typeface, Gill Sans, on a black background. Using only one weight and one font in both compositions I created a contrast and active negative space through kerning, leading, tracking, and grouping.
